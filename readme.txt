This widget will show Kaskus Hot Threads on your sidebar.

== Description ==

Kaskus is the largest online community in Indonesia having total users of more than two million. Around 227 million posts are produced on the site. Kaskus s listed in 1,000 world's top sites arranged by Google AdPlanner released in August of 2010. Compared to July, Kaskus hikes over 216 levels from 755 to 539.

Every day, Kaskus will choose the most interested/unique threads as Hot Threads. So, this widget will show Kaskus Hot Threads on your wordpres blog's sidebar.


== Installation ==

1. Upload this plugin to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Place Kaskus Hot Threads widget to sidebar.
